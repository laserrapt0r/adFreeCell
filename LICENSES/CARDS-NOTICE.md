## How adFreeCell uses it (and stays compliant)

- The **complete, unmodified** per-card sources ship with this repository under
  [`assets/cards/`](../assets/cards/) (`1c.svg` … `13s.svg`). These are the
  "source code" of the artwork in the LGPL sense.
- For offline / `file://` use, those same cards are embedded verbatim in
  [`js/cards-sprite.js`](../js/cards-sprite.js), generated by
  [`tools/build-cards.js`](../tools/build-cards.js). The only change made while
  embedding is a mechanical **namespacing of the internal ids** (`suit`,
  `numeral`) per card so all 52 cards can coexist in one document — the card
  designs themselves are untouched.
- adFreeCell references individual cards by id via SVG `<use>` (for example
  `card_1s` = Ace of Spades, `card_13h` = King of Hearts). This is dynamic use
  of the library and does not create a derivative of the artwork.
- Because the LGPL library is provided in full and unmodified, and this notice
  plus the license texts accompany the distribution, the requirements of the
  LGPL-3.0 are satisfied while the adFreeCell game code remains under its own
  MIT license.

If you fork or redistribute adFreeCell, **keep `assets/cards/`, this notice and
the LGPL/GPL texts in place.**

## Card id naming (for reference)

- Source files & sprite ids: `{rank}{suit}` — rank `1`(Ace) … `13`(King),
  suit `c` club, `d` diamond, `h` heart, `s` spade.
- Sprite group ids are prefixed with `card_` (e.g. `card_10h`, `card_12s`).
- A `joker.svg` also exists in the upstream deck but is unused by the game.
- Natural card size: 225 × 314.
