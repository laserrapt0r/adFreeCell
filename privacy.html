Privacy Policy — adFreeCell

Last updated: July 2026

Short version: adFreeCell collects nothing, sends nothing and shows no ads. There are no accounts, no analytics, no trackers and no third-party services.

What data is stored

The game stores a small amount of data locally on your device using your browser's localStorage:

  • your settings (language, theme, sound, auto-collect, timer visibility);
  • your statistics (games played and won, streaks, best time, fewest moves);
  • the game currently in progress, so you can resume it later.

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted anywhere. You can erase it at any time via Settings → Statistics → Reset, or by clearing your browser's site data.

Network

adFreeCell is a static, self-contained app. After the files have loaded (or been installed from the Play Store / as a Progressive Web App), it runs entirely offline and makes no network requests on its own. There is no server that receives any information about you. The only exception is if you choose to open the optional tip link (see below).

Donations (optional)

The app offers an entirely optional link to leave a tip via PayPal. Nothing is sent unless you tap it. Choosing it opens PayPal in your browser; any payment is then handled entirely by PayPal under its own privacy policy. adFreeCell neither sees nor stores any payment information.

Ads & tracking

None. adFreeCell contains no advertising, no advertising SDKs, no analytics and no tracking of any kind. That is the whole point of the name.

Children

The game is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children.
